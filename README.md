# 🚖 Uber Ride Booking Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze Uber ride booking data and provide actionable business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor booking performance, revenue trends, vehicle utilization, payment preferences, customer ratings, and operational efficiency through dynamic visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project transforms raw Uber ride booking data into an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard using **Power BI**. It provides a comprehensive overview of ride bookings, revenue, cancellations, customer behavior, and vehicle performance, helping businesses make informed, data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall booking performance.
- Monitor revenue and ride distance.
- Track completed, cancelled, and incomplete rides.
- Identify preferred payment methods.
- Compare vehicle-wise performance.
- Analyze monthly and quarterly booking trends.
- Evaluate customer and driver ratings.
- Identify top pickup and drop locations.

## 📌 Key Insights

- 📍 **149K** total ride bookings analyzed.
- 💰 Generated **₹52 Million** in revenue.
- ✅ **93K** rides completed successfully.
- 🚗 Total ride distance exceeded **2.51 Million** units.
- ⭐ Customer Rating: **4.40/5**
- ⭐ Driver Rating: **4.23/5**
- 💳 **UPI** emerged as the most preferred payment method.
- 🚕 **Auto** contributed the highest share of total revenue (**25%**).
- 📍 Highest pickup location: **Khandsa**
- 📍 Highest drop location: **Ashram**
